Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

No data collected No tracking No account required Fully on-device

01

Introduction

This app is designed with a strict privacy-first philosophy. Your personal data is not collected, tracked, or shared. All core functionality operates entirely on your device.

02

No Account Required

You can use the app without creating an account. We do not request or store names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers.

03

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data. The app does not track your identity, usage across apps, or browsing behavior.

04

On-Device Processing

All features, including voice-based dhikr counting, are processed entirely on your device. Audio input is used only in real-time for functionality and is never stored or transmitted.

05

Local Data Storage

Your dhikr counts, sessions, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data remains under your control and is not sent to any external servers.

06

App Group Data Sharing

The app may use Apple's App Group storage to share data securely between the main app, widgets, and Apple Watch extension. This data remains on your device and is not shared externally.

07

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect behavioral data or usage analytics unless explicitly stated and opted into by the user.

08

In-App Purchases

The app offers a one-time purchase to unlock premium features. All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details or billing information.

09

Permissions

The app may request access to certain device features: microphone (for voice-based dhikr counting) and notifications (for reminders). These permissions are used solely for core functionality and are never used for tracking or data collection.

10

Widgets and Apple Watch

Widgets and Apple Watch features access data stored locally on your device. No information is transmitted externally when using these features.

11

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party advertising networks, analytics providers, or external data processors.

12

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transfer your data to any third parties.

13

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device and remains there until you delete it. Deleting the app will remove all associated data from your device.

14

User Control

You have full control over your data. You may clear or delete your data at any time by removing the app or resetting your device.

15

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any users.

16

Security

Since data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from the security mechanisms provided by iOS, including device encryption and system-level protections.

17

International Use

As no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfers.

18

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised 'Last Updated' date.

19

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: support@yourapp.com
